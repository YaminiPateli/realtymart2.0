Everything you need to know about title check

What is a legal title check?

A legal title check is a thorough examination conducted to validate property ownership and ensure that the property is unencumbered by any legal encumbrances or obligations.

What is the role of a legal expert?

A seasoned legal professional conducts a comprehensive investigation into the property, collating a wealth of information. Their responsibilities encompass scrutinizing and confirming property titles, delving into real estate records, and compiling detailed records of mortgages, contracts, and other pertinent documents. This meticulous process guarantees that the property in question remains unburdened by any legal encumbrances.

When you should opt for a legal title search?

Prior to the purchase of a residential or commercial property, the significance of performing a title search cannot be overstated. It is a pivotal step to confirm that the property stands devoid of any legal disputes and possesses an unblemished title, devoid of any claims, liens, or potential issues that might lead to complications if left unchecked.

What information is covered in the legal title check report?

A comprehensive legal title check report unveils a wealth of information pertaining to property taxes, potential deed documentation discrepancies, any lingering ownership disputes, and the legal authority of the current owner to transfer the property.

What is the process of title check and how long does it take?

A professional legal title check, administered by a legal specialist, involves a comprehensive review of historical records and the present status of the property. It encompasses confirming whether any mortgages or claims are associated with the property. Additionally, it ensures that the current seller possesses the unencumbered authority to transfer the property. This meticulous process typically spans a timeframe of 7 to 10 days.
